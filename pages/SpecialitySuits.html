Specialty Suites

Extraordinary levels of luxury and service at Turtle Valley

Our unique Specialty Suite is the perfect retreat for families, couples, or larger groups looking for extra space to stretch out. Exuding modern luxury, this contemporary one-bedroom suite features a spacious living room with a cozy dining area, a butler's pantry with a sink and microwave, a private workspace with custom furnishings inspired by Sri Lankan heritage, and exclusive access to Horizon Club privileges.
