Sip & Savor at Our Signature Bars

Unwind in style at our two uniquely designed bars offering signature cocktails, premium spirits, and breathtaking views.

Infinity Bar

Perched by the infinity pool, this stylish open-air bar offers an extensive selection of international wines, spirits, and handcrafted cocktails. Enjoy the relaxed poolside atmosphere with ambient music and an unbeatable sunset view.

  • Location: Pool Deck, Ground Level
  • Hours: 12:00 PM – 11:00 PM
  • Specialties: Sunset Mojito, Mango Passion Daiquiri, Premium Gin Flights
  • Atmosphere: Chill-out lounge with sea breeze and candlelit tables

Velvet Lounge

A classy, indoor bar with a velvet-accented interior, live piano music, and a refined selection of aged whiskies, rare wines, and bespoke cocktails. Perfect for evening meetups or a relaxed nightcap in elegant surroundings.

  • Location: Lobby Level
  • Hours: 5:00 PM – 2:00 AM
  • Specialties: Smoked Old Fashioned, Ceylon Negroni, Single Malt Whisky Collection
  • Atmosphere: Elegant, with velvet seating and low lighting
