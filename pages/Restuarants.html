Discover Our Restaurants

Four distinctive dining destinations inside Turtle Valley, each offering a unique culinary journey.

Ocean Breeze Grill

Overlooking the Indian Ocean, Ocean Breeze Grill specializes in fresh seafood grilled to perfection. Enjoy signature dishes such as grilled jumbo prawns, seared tuna steaks, and our famous crab curry. The ambiance is casual luxury— ideal for romantic dinners or relaxed sunset meals.

  • Location: Poolside, Ground Level
  • Hours: 12:00 PM – 10:30 PM
  • Cuisine: Seafood, Mediterranean
  • Seating: Indoor & Oceanfront Terrace

Spice Garden

Dive into the authentic flavors of Sri Lanka at Spice Garden. With an open kitchen and traditional clay pot cooking, this restaurant celebrates local ingredients and centuries-old recipes like hoppers, kottu, and jackfruit curry.

  • Location: Lobby Level
  • Hours: 11:00 AM – 3:00 PM, 6:00 PM – 10:00 PM
  • Cuisine: Sri Lankan & South Indian
  • Seating: Garden Veranda

Sunset Sky Lounge

This rooftop lounge offers breathtaking city and ocean views, artisan cocktails, and a tapas-style menu. Perfect for sundowners and late-night vibes, enjoy live jazz every weekend and signature drinks like the Cinnamon Spice Mule and Tamarind Margarita.

  • Location: Rooftop, Level 10
  • Hours: 5:00 PM - 1:00 AM
  • Cuisine: Tapas, Cocktails
  • Seating: Lounge & Sky Deck

The Garden Café

Start your day with artisanal coffee and freshly baked pastries at The Garden Café. Offering all-day brunch, fresh smoothies, and vegan delights, this casual spot is nestled in our lush tropical courtyard.

  • Location: Courtyard Level
  • Hours: 7:00 AM - 6:00 PM
  • Cuisine: Café, Healthy, Vegan-friendly
  • Seating: Outdoor Garden Tables
