Adventure Zone

Get ready for action-packed experiences! Whether you're a thrill-seeking adult or a playful youngster, our Adventure Zone offers unforgettable fun for everyone.

Junior Jungle

A colorful wonderland for kids packed with creative and exciting activities. From mini obstacle courses to bouncy castles and indoor rock climbing, it’s the perfect spot for young adventurers to explore safely.

  • Age Group: 3 – 12 years
  • Activities: Climbing wall, Soft play zone, Trampoline park, Mini zipline
  • Supervision: Staff-monitored playtime for extra safety
  • Hours: 9:00 AM – 6:00 PM
  • Location: Level 1, Adventure Wing

Extreme Thrill Park

For grown-up adrenaline junkies, this zone delivers high-energy challenges and outdoor fun. Conquer the zipline, take on the rope course, or go head-to-head in competitive obstacle games!

  • Age Group: 13+ years
  • Activities: Giant zipline, High-rope courses, Paintball arena, Archery tag
  • Gear: Safety equipment provided
  • Hours: 10:00 AM – 7:00 PM
  • Location: Outdoor Adventure Grounds
