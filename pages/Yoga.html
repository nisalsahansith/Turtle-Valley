Yoga Club

Discover inner peace and physical harmony at the Turtle Valley Yoga Club, a tranquil haven for yogis of all levels. Guided by certified instructors, our sessions blend traditional wisdom with modern techniques in a serene, nature-kissed setting.

Turtle Valley Yoga Club

Immerse yourself in a holistic wellness experience designed to rejuvenate your body and calm your mind. Our open-air yoga pavilion, surrounded by lush tropical gardens, provides the perfect ambiance for meditation, mindful breathing, and physical renewal.

What We Offer:
  • Daily Sessions: Sunrise Flow, Hatha Yoga, Vinyasa, Restorative Yoga, Evening Meditation
  • Private Classes: One-on-one or small group sessions tailored to your needs
  • Workshops: Monthly wellness retreats and yoga philosophy classes
  • Instructors: Internationally certified yoga teachers with multi-style training
  • Ambiance: Nature-inspired yoga deck, calming music, herbal tea lounge
  • Equipment: Mats, bolsters, straps, and blocks provided
Timing & Access:
  • Opening Hours: 6:30 AM – 8:00 PM daily
  • Location: Garden Pavilion, Wellness Wing
  • Access: Complimentary for all resort guests. Day passes available for non-guests.
Special Wellness Touch:
  • Complimentary detox water and herbal infusions after each session
  • Yoga Club gift pack for members staying 3 nights or more
  • Mindfulness journals and affirmation cards available upon request
