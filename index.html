About

Nestled along the breathtaking shores of the Indian Ocean, Turtle Valley is a true sanctuary of elegance and tranquility in the heart of Galle. Overlooking the stunning coastline, our luxurious retreat offers a seamless blend of modern sophistication and warm hospitality.

Unwind in our spacious, elegantly designed rooms and suites, each offering panoramic ocean or city views, plush furnishings, and state-of-the-art amenities for an unforgettable stay. Immerse yourself in pure relaxation at our infinity pool, where the shimmering waters meet the endless horizon, or rejuvenate your senses at our world-class spa and wellness center

Savor an exquisite culinary journey at our award-winning restaurants, where global flavors and local delicacies come together in a delightful symphony of taste. For the ultimate shopping experience, explore Arpico super center, featuring a curated selection of premium brands and bespoke boutiques.

Whether you're planning a grand celebration or an intimate gathering, our spacious event venues set the stage for unforgettable moments, with impeccable service and stunning backdrops.

At Turtle Valley, every stay is a story of indulgence, relaxation, and unparalleled luxury. Discover a world of serenity and sophistication in the heart of Galle.
